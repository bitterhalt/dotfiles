# Dotfiles
This is where I keep all of my dotfiles for apps that I use daily. Feel free to copy any of the dotfiles or scripts you find here, just like I "stole" some from others. ;) 

Do not try to copy/paste my entire setup because it is very disorganized and some of stuff might not work in your system. Instead, try copying bits and pieces here and there so you put together your own dotfiles unique to you.
